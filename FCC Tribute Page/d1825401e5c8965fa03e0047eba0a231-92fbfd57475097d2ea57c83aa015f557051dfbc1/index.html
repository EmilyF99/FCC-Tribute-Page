Sir Dr.Stephen Hawking

English theoretical physicist, cosmologist, and author

An image of Sir Dr.Stephen

A brief timeline of Sir Dr.Hawkings life

  • 1942 - Sir Stephen Hawkins is born in Oxford England.
  • 1952 - Attends St. Albans School.
  • 1959 - Receives scholarship to attend the University of Oxford, he graduates with a degree in Natural Science.
  • 1962 - Begins graduate research in cosmology at Cambridge University.
  • 1963 - Diagnosed with the degenerative nerve disorder ALS.
  • 1965 - Marries his first wife, Jane Wilde.
  • 1967 - First son, Robert is born.
  • 1970 - Daughter named Lucy is born.
  • 1974 - Elected as a fellow of the Royal Society at age 32, one of the youngest people to receive the honor.
  • 1979 - Becomes Lucasian Professor of Mathematics at Cambridge. Second son, Timothy is born.
  • 1988 - Publishes “A Brief History of Time”.
  • 1989 - Made a Companion of Honor by Queen Elizabeth II.
  • 1995 - Marries his second wife, Eliaine Mason
  • 2014 - Hawking’s life is celebrated in the Oscar-winning “The Theory of Everything”.
  • 2018 - Stephen Hawking passes away aged 76.
